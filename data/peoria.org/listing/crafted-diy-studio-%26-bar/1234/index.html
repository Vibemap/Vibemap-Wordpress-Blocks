We are a unique space located in Peoria's vibrant Warehouse District. We are a DIY Studio AND a bar, so anyone is welcome to come in and have a drink! Here you can handcraft a beautiful project while sipping on your beverage of choice - let those creative juices flow! Grab your friends, choose your project and we'll provide the rest - all tools, materials, and instructions are ready for you to create your very own masterpiece. And if crafting is not your thing just pop in the front for one of our handcrafted cocktails, craft beers, or wine. Drink in the ambiance of our makers' space as you drink up one of our delicious libations! The CRAFT & DRINK Experience Make a reservation to join us for a Craft & Drink session, walk-ins are also welcome when room allows. Each session is 3 hours long and we recommend you arrive on time. We have 15+ projects to choose from, they each take between 1 - 3 hours to complete (no experience necessary!). -- from www.crafteddiystudio.com
