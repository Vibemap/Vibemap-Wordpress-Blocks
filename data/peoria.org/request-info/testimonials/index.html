Meeting Testimonials

"The success of our 2019 Collie Club of America National Specialty Dog Show cannot be talked about without mentioning the contributions of our real Hosts; the City of Peoria, the Peoria Civic Center, the Marriott Pere Marquette & the Courtyard by Marriott, and the Peoria Area Convention & Visitors Bureau. They were all an absolute joy to work with! They took our "Collie Invasion" in stride and worked with us every step of the way to accommodate our every need."

Jean Framke
Collie Club of America
Show Co-Chair

 

"Holding the Illinois Music Education Conference in Peoria has worked for ILMEA for over 30 years due, in no small part, to the amazing work of the Peoria Area Convention and Visitors Bureau!  No question or concern has been too great or too small, and the staff is always available to assist us with creative and workable solutions.  ILMEA could not produce our event without the Peoria CVB!"

Darcy A. Nendza, CMP
Illinois Music Education Association
Executive Director

 

 

Our group recently needed the assistance of the PACVB while prepping our bid to host the 2019 Collie Club of America National Specialty Dog Show at the Peoria Civic Center. We were amazed when they outlined all aspects of what they could do for us.

The PACVB provided us with an array of needed information on the Peoria area, proposals from area hotels, airport information, and they worked closely with the PCC to apply for and secure funding for our group. Additionally, as we were short on time at that point, did so in an extremely timely and courteous manner! They then went above and beyond, in our opinion, by compiling all the info into an exceptional Packet that they allowed us to customize by including any other documents we desired. The end result of their hard work gave us an outstanding, professional addition to our bid.  They were so easy to work with and BOY! did they make us look good!  We needed to beat out a competing bid by impressing 52 Board members!

I believe part of our amazement at the service we received was due to our strained dealing with another venue in the Chicago/Northern Illinois area and total lack of assistance from their CVB.  WOW what a difference! Laura, Cory & Kim at the PACVB made us feel welcome from the very start!  And when we came down for a visit we were further impressed by their hospitality! We not only toured the PCC and Marriott hotels, but Laura & Kim surprised us and drove us around town too!  We could ask them anything and received friendly, prompt, detailed, if we dont know well find out, we are in this together responses!  Their seamless relationship with the PCC was also refreshing. We absolutely know they have our backs and with their help we know we can and will make our event One of a Kind!!  

Jean Franke and Kelly Montgomery
2019 Host Co-Chairs
Collie Club of America
