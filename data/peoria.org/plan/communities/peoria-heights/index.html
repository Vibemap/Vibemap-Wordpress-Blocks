Village of Peoria Heights

Eat•Shop•Play
Uniquely Boutique

Nestled on the west bluffs of the Illinois River, the Village of Peoria Heights possesses City convenience with Village charm.

It is home to a thriving downtown business district that hosts over seventy-five eclectic and independently owned retail stores and restaurants.

Peoria Heights is famous for Tower Park which hosts a one-of-a-kind 200 ft. observation tower. The tower provides gorgeous panoramic views of the village, the Illinois River Valley and its surrounding areas.

Being home to Forest Park Nature Center, the Rock Island Greenway, and Grandview Drive makes Peoria Heights perfect for any active lifestyle.

Teddy Roosevelt named Grandview Drive the “World’s Most Beautiful Drive” in 1910 for a reason, as it has the most breathtaking views of the river and many historic and remarkable estates lining it.

We invite you to explore this quaint Village; you will feel right at home in the thriving and artsy community.
