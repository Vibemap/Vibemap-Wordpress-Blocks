Louisville Slugger Sports Complex

 

Baseball, softball, soccer, or volleyball... LSSC offers a modern and unique experience to all who pass through its gates. LSSC hosts hundreds of teams throughout the year for world-class fast-pitch tournaments, including the USSSA Great Lakes National Championships and USSSA Illinois State Championships.

2023 USSSA Sports Complex of the Year!

Whether you’re looking for premier playing surfaces, great spectator seating, or excellent concessions; you can expect a a state-of-the-art facility with a hometown feel.

Home Run!

Featuring twelve synthetic turf fields and a 125,000-square-foot dome. The stadium seats 1,300 people, making it the perfect place for tournaments, leagues, clinics, showcases, and events across multiple sports.
