309-692-7810

Mt. Hawley C.C. is a Private Members Club in Peoria, IL. Our family-friendly facilities with vista views of the Illinois River include an 18-hole golf course, three swimming pools, a pool cabana, four tennis courts, two pickleball courts, two bocce ball courts, casual, formal and banquet dining, plus social events year round! Established in 1922, Mt. Hawley C.C. is now 100 years old!
