City of Washington

Historic Washington is a wonderful place to slow down and enjoy life. Immerse yourself in the unique and charming shopping opportunities on historic Washington Square. Grab a drink at Faire, a delicious cafe located in a renovated bank from the 1800s.

Visit Five Points Washington, a community center for all ages. In addition to the fitness and aquatic center, Five Points offers banquet and meeting space and theater. The event calendar is always filled with exciting, nationally recognized acts you won’t want to miss.

Washington invites you to come and enjoy the quaint shops, flavorsome dining, and relaxed atmosphere of their historic downtown square. Enjoy ice cream and popcorn from Holland’s Caramel Corn, and buy a unique gift at Sentimental Journey.

Make sure to plan for a wine tasting at Tres Rojas Winery or make a stop at the popular The Blend coffee shop before trying out your favorite new bike at Russell's Cycling and Fitness store.

Washington Good Neighbor Days happens every summer throughout the city, with a variety of festival-related events.
