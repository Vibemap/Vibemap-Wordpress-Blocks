16 Revolutionary Ways To Celebrate 2022 Independence Day

By Discover Peoria on Jun. 28, 2022

This Fourth of July is booming with exciting events to celebrate Independence Day in Greater Peoria. 

Prepare yourself for a long weekend of patriotic music, tri-corner hats, stars and stripes, cold beverages, barbeques, and fireworks! Bring out the red, white, and blue for the 4th of July, as we honor Independence Day with these 16 revolutionary Peoria Area celebrations:

 

Concert for the City:  FT. Jordan Feliz and Victor Solomon

Saturday July 2, 2022   //   6-10pm

Dozer ParkDowntown Peoria

Admission:  $25 per ticket

 

God Bless America:  A Patriotic Gospelfest

Saturday July 2, 2022   //   7-8:30pm

Five Points WashingtonWashington

Admission:  FREE

 

Mason County Fireworks 2022

Saturday July 2, 2022   //   10am-dusk

Riverfront ParkHavana

Admission:  FREE

 

Peoria Speedway Fireworks

Saturday July 2, 2022   //   3pm-dusk

Peoria SpeedwayPeoria

Admission:  Adults $10, Kids 10-Under free

 

Glen Oak Park Fireworks

Sunday, July 3, 2022   //   8pm-dusk

Glen Oak ParkPeoria

Admission:  $3

 

Morton Community Independence Day Celebration

Sunday, July 3, 2022   //   dusk

McClallen ParkMorton

Admission:  FREE

 

4th of July Honor America Celebration at Memorial Stadium

Monday, July 4, 2022   //   8-10pm

Memorial StadiumPekin

Admission:  Ages 13 and up $5 per person, Ages 6-12 $1 per person, ages five and under are free, Veterans and active military personnel receive free admission

 

Chillicothe July 4th

Monday, July 4, 2022   //   5:30pm-dusk

Three Sisters ParkChillicothe

Admission:  FREE

 

Elmwood Fireworks

Monday, July 4, 2022   //   dusk

Sweetwater ParkElmwood 

Admission:  FREE

 

Food Truck Frenzy & Fireworks at Eureka Lake

Monday, July 4, 2022   //   10am-11pm

Eureka LakeEureka

Admission:  FREE

 

20th Annual Fourth of July Family Fun Fest:  Boys and Girls Club of Greater Peoria fundraiser

Monday, July 4, 2022   //   5pm-dusk

Kelleher's Irish PubDowntown Peoria

Admission:  $250 for 8 adults, children 5 and under are freeincludes two parking passes and food

 

Peoria Chiefs Game at Dozer Park

Monday, July 4, 2022   //   5:35pm

Dozer ParkDowntown Peoria

Admission:  Tickets start at $12

 

Red White and Boom

Monday, July 4, 2022   //   6pm-dusk

Peoria + East Peoria RiverfrontPeoria and East Peoria

Admission:  FREE
