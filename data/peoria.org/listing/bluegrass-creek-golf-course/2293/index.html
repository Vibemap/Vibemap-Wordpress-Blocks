Bluegrass Creek Golf Course

10521 Boston School Rd
Minier, IL 61759

(309) 392-2094

Visit Website
1 of 1
Share

Bluegrass Creek Golf Course is a beautiful 9-hole course in the country. The course has no sand traps, no rough, no Geese, and best of all, NO TEE TIMES. Built from a cow pasture in 1962, Bluegrass Creek traces the natural movement of the land and provides 360-degree views of Tazewell County.
