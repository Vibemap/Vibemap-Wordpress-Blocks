Shop Marshall County

By Discover Peoria on Nov. 08, 2022

If you're the wandering type who aimlessly strolls through the aisles with an iced latte in hand and touching, well, almost everything, then we have the list for you. Shopping lovers unite—check out these locally-owned boutique shops in Marshall County.


 

HENRY

Fiber Faire  {Henry}    //    Fiber Faire offers sewing machine repair and service along with long-arm quilting services and classes. 

 

Henry Hometown Hardware  {Henry}    //    This locally owned hardware shop specializes in and sells all the tools you need to finish any DIY home improvement projects.

 

Occasional Treasures  {Henry}    //    This vintage shop sells new, used, and antique items like furniture, home decor, and kitchen essentials.


 

LACON

Lela’s Resale Shoppe  {Lacon}    //    Lela’s Resale Shoppe is the place to go to find new and used furniture. Fun fact: if you’re looking for a specific piece, the team here will be happy to find it for you.

 

Midwest Farm Charm  {Lacon}    //    Stop by Midwest Farm Charm to secure all of your modern farmhouse home decor and stylish clothing. 

 

Small Town Girls Boutique  {Lacon}    //    Small Town Girls is a charming boutique specializing in trendy women’s clothing and accessories.


 

TOLUCA

Miller’s Hardware  {Toluca}    //    Miller’s Hardware is your quintessential small-town hardware shop with quality tools and everything needed to finish any home improvement project.


 

WENONA

Ogden Creative Designs  {Wenona}    //    Whether you’re looking for custom clothing or custom SWAG, Ogden Creative Designs can personalize any item imaginable.
