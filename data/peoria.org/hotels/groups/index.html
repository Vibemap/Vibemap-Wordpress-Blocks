Group Hotels

No matter if you need lodging for a bachelorette party, wedding, sports team or business meeting, many of the hotels in the Peoria area offer group accommodations to satisfy all members of your party.

Consider Peoria Marriott Pere Marquette if you’re looking for a sophisticated and elegant stay with an on-site restaurant and luxurious amenities. Embassy Suites by Hilton is a relaxed all-suite hotel with a indoor pool, cafe, restaurant and conference center that offers group booking.

Stay at Holiday Inn Express and Suites for laid back rooms, an indoor pool, a gym, and free breakfast. Mark Twain Hotel is a boutique style hotel and another option for groups with free airport transportation and an onsite restaurant.

If you’re seeking a comfortable and satisfactory stay for a large group, central Illinois will not let you down!
