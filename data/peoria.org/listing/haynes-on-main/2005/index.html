Haynes on Main

108 S Main Street
Mackinaw, IL 61755

(309) 359-6246

Visit Website

We're Haynes on Main and we're a local bar and grill here in Mackinaw, IL. We believe in good food and good times, offering a full bar, dine-in, and carry-out options. We started this business to provide our community with a great place to gather with friends and family for drinks and fantastic food. We're looking forward to growing our business to continue to serve Mackinaw and the surrounding areas. We'd like to personally invite you to stop in today for pizza, smash burgers, sandwiches, beer, and more!
