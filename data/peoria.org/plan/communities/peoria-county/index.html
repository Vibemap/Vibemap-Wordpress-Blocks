Peoria County

Known as the Heart of Illinois, Peoria County lies on the west side of the Illinois River in halfway between Chicago, IL and St. Louis, MO. Known for having big city amenities in a small-town feel Peoria Country has 15 cities and villages for you to discover. Read on to learn how Peoria County is brimming with possibility- with connections and relationships found around every corner of this tight-knit community.
