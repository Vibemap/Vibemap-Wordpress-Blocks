The Shoppes at Grand Prairie 

Out on the North-end of town, you can find the open-aired and modern outdoor shopping destination, The Shoppes at Grand Prairie.  Take a stroll among the park-like landscape, take a photo with one of the murals or statues, and shop all day at featured popular stores like HomeGoods, Loft and Dicks Sporting Goods. In addition to the many name-brand retailers, the Shoppes at Grand Prairie also features more than 20 specialty stores that are exclusive to the Peoria market.

Before you shop, grab a coffee from Faire Coffee, a local and women-owned coffee shop with unique flavors such as Lavender Peppermint and Rose Brown Sugar Cardamom. If youre looking for fashionable Womens clothing, stop into Drygoods for all the newest trends. Visit White House Black Market for upscale Womens clothing.

Find all of your favorite name brands at great prices for the whole family at Marshalls, or check out DSW Designer Shoe Warehouse for shoes for every age and occasion. Check out Eddie Bauer for activewear, apparel, and gear.

If you get hungry while shopping, youre in luck! The Shoppes at Grand Prairie has many yummy local eats nearby. Childers Eatery is a local favorite that serves a variety of tasty breakfast and lunch options. Osaka Hibachi & Sushi Bar is also known for its delicious hibachi meals that are cooked right in front of you, and for its number of sushi roll options. Pancheros will satisfy any burrito craving, serving up burritos so well-built they can stand by themselves. Don't forget to visit Genghis Grill to create your own stir fry bowl!
