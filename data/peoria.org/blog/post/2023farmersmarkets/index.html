Greater Peoria Farmers Markets

By Discover Peoria on Apr. 06, 2023

Is anyone else a sucker for farmers markets? 

We love exploring, finding new flavors, and saying hello to friendly faces. There is happiness in a bag overflowing with convenient farm-fresh produce, affordable products, and handmade treats. But moreso, there is immense joy in linking our urban, suburban, and rural Peoria Area communities through the bountiful production of our agriculture, makers, and farmers at our regional markets.

 

Nothing beets local treats! Romaine calm, we have just the spot for you—turnup for these quintessential Greater Peoria markets:
