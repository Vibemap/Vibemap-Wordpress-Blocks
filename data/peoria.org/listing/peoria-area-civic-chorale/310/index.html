The Peoria Area Civic Chorale (PACC) exists to develop, create, produce, and perform a diverse repertoire of the highest quality vocal and choral music presented in the most professional manner for the enjoyment, entertainment, aesthetic enrichment, and education of its members, its audiences, and those who live in the communities it serves. There are two concert series each year featuring adult and youth chorus groups from grades 2-8 and a young artist competition for High School students.
