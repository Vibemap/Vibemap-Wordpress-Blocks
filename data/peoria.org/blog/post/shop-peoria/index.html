Shop Peoria County // Part 1

By Discover Peoria on Nov. 29, 2022

If you're the wandering type who aimlessly strolls through the aisles with an iced latte in hand and touching, well, almost everything, then we have the list for you. Shopping lovers unitecheck out these locally-owned boutique shops in Peoria County.


 

PEORIA

Apricot Lane Boutique  {Peoria}    //    Shop Apricot Lane Boutique Peoria for the latest women's fashion apparel.

 

Blue Boutique  {Peoria}    //    Blue Boutique specializes in quality items like clothing, accessories, gifts, and more

 

Bushbaby  {Peoria}   //   This online-only boutique specializes in children's outdoor apparel and footwear.

 

Bushwhacker  {Peoria}   //   Established in 1971, Bushwhacker is Peorias original outdoor store. Originally a backpacking shop for hippies, now they've become the local escape to unknown adventure.

 

Indie Gift House  {Peoria}   //  The makers of Hello Headband bring you a boutique specializing in all things giftscandles to candy and towels to quirky stickers.

 

Kelly Seed  {Peoria}   //  With a complete line of alfalfas, clovers, pasture mixes, waterway mixes, lawn mixes, and garden seeds, Kellys can take care of the Central Illinois farmer and homeowner alike.

 

Noelle Rose Boutique  {Peoria}   //  Trendy, Boho inspired shop with clothes and accessories. 

 

Pink Sugar Boutique   {Peoria}   //  Exclusive brands from casual everyday styles to that special event dress that make women look and feel their best. Featuring brands such as Sanctuary, Free People, BB Dakota, DL 1961, Cupcakes and Cashmere, and French Connection. 

 

Rambler  {Peoria}    //    Rambler is a moto-outdoors inspired men's specialty shop located in the Warehouse District. With a focus on quality, American, adventure-ready goods, this man-boutique is a great spot to explore.

 

Random Boutique  {Peoria}   //  The place to go when you want to find something out of the ordinary, Random Boutique has an array of fun and funky clothes. 

 

Relics  {Peoria}   //  Gift shop aiming to bring interesting things to Peoria that tell a story. Relics carries jewelry, cards, books, decor, vintage surprises, and plants.

 

Running Central  {Peoria}   //  More than just a running store. Seeking to offer comfort and enjoyment for every occasion whether you are a mother, father, grandparent, or a walker, runner, or fitness enthusiast.

 

Uniquee Tees Inc  {Peoria}  //  Uniquee Tees specializes in custom embroidery and screen printing. Located in Campustown next to Bradley University in Peoria, Uniquee Tees is the perfect place for all of your custom T-shirt needs.

 

Urban Artifacts  {Peoria}   //   Rescue, repurpose, recycle is the name of the game at Urban Artifacts. Check out this treasure chest of antiques, nostalgic pieces, souvenirs, and vintage items.

 

Wishing Star Boutique by Tabatha  {Peoria}    //    Wishing Star Boutique is the Peoria Area's headquarters for the latest trends in fashion! Channeling Southern vibes with Midwestern flair, WSB dresses the fashionista, the casual gal, and the working woman on the go.


 

PEORIA HEIGHTS

A Perfect Pear  {Peoria Heights}    //    Think of Perfect Pear Boutique as a locally-owned Nordstrom or Neiman-Marcus. The boutique offers the same designer lines coveted in metropolitan cities like New York, Los Angeles, and Chicago. 

Generations Candle Company  {Peoria Heights}    //    Generations offers custom fragrances as a unique experience. Create your own candle fragrance or purchase one premade. 


Global Village  {Peoria Heights}   //   Global Village is a local, all-volunteer, not-for-profit store providing Fair Trade food and gifts for a better world. Check out this shop in the Heights today for unique gifts and treasures.


I Know You Like A Book  {Peoria Heights}    //    I Know You Like a Book is an independent bookstore offering new books, used books and reading-related gift items. Serving the literary Central Illinois community for over 15 years.


Sage and Co  {Peoria Heights}    //    A size inclusive lifestyle store that offers clothing, home decor, self care, and more.
 

Shabby Shac  {Peoria Heights}    //    Shabby Shac offers repurposed, Upcycle, Vintage, Antique, Arts/Crafts, Shabby and uniquely outside the box items.


The Nook  {Peoria Heights, Germantown Hills, + Morton}    //    The Nook is your one stop shop for great gifts, beautiful decor, fun accessories, and on-trend apparel.

 

So Chic  {Peoria Heights + Morton}    //    With two locations you can find the perfect stationery, party supplies, decor, and more at So Chic. They also offer party planning services!


 

WEST PEORIA

Primitiques Market on Main {West Peoria}    //  Furniture store with unique pieces for every home. 


 

CHILLICOTHE

Covered Wagon Crafts  {Chillicothe}    //   Covered Wagon Crafts is a Arts and Crafts store located in Chillicothe, Illinois. For over 30 years, this shop has offered art + craft supplies and collectors items. 

 

Cranberry Creek  {Chillicothe}    //   Cranberry Creek specializes in hand-poured soy candles and hand-crafted natural bath and body products, Essential oils, home decor, clothing and accessories. They produce their soap in small batches to guarantee that each bar of soap is top quality.

 

Little Shop of Hoarders  {Chillicothe}   //   A fun "Re-Sell Re-tell" shop full of usual and unusual treasures! Antiques, Art, Vintage, Furniture, Sculptures, Book Nook, Sporting Goods, Home Decor, Seasonal Items, and much more

 

Looking 4 Treasures  {Chillicothe}  //  Looking 4 Treasures is a thrift and consignment store offering quality used furniture, small appliances, a big selection of used books, tools, sports items, collectibles, primitives, pottery, pictures, and glassware.

 

Peggy Says Sew  {Chillicothe}    //   Full service seamstress to fit all of your sewing and alterations needs. 

 

Sweet Finds  {Chillicothe}   //   An exciting new take on the traditional shopping experience, with deals that couldn't even be found online. Unexpected finds and sweet deals on an array of items from guitars and headphones to sweaters and shoes. 

 

The Princess Closet  {Chillicothe}    //   Dedicated to promoting confidence in young adults by striving to help them attend their formal events in style. Choose from over 100 new or gently used dresses!


 

BARTONVILLE

Ever Louise Boutique  {Bartonville}   //   This adorable boutique in Bartonville specializes in stylish fashions for every season. With new arrivals weekly, follow Ever Louise Boutique on social media for amazing steals and deals.

 

Faith + Grace Childrens Boutique  {Bartonville}    //   A childrens boutique that creates a unique one-of-a-kind shopping experience that captures the look and vibe of the hot trends.  The boutique is locally owned, and all the styles are hand-picked by the owner.

 

Feeling Chic Boutique  {Bartonville}    //   Offers beautiful, trendy, and affordable clothing that looks amazing on all body types. They work hard to pick pieces that will flatter all body shapes and are quality fabrics!


 

BRIMFIELD

Brimfield Hardware  {Brimfield}    //   Brimfield Hardware is your reliable source for home repair and remodeling products. Since 2000, theyve been the proud supplier for homeowners, contractors, and farmers in Brimfield.


 

PRINCEVILLE

EB Buildings and Lumber Co.  {Princeville}    //    A full-service lumberyard, post frame building business, and retail hardware home center.


 

ELMWOOD

New 2U Community Thrift Store  {Elmwood}    //    Consignment and thrift store specializing in gently used clothing items.


 

HANNA CITY

Country Relics  {Hanna City}    //    Country gifts, primitives, candles, seasonal dcor, rugs, textiles, antiques and lots more.

 

South Eden Village Mall  {Hanna City}    //    A gift, furniture and antique mall filled with finds from the past and fun, beautiful, and useful items. 


 

EDWARDS

The Fashion Farm Boutique  {Farmington}    //   Owned by a fun and loving mom with a passion for fashion and helping other women feel beautiful! We are a womens boutique here for your fashion, accessory and gift needs!


 

KICKAPOO

The Fashion Farm Boutique Kickapoo {Edwards + Kickapoo}   //   The Fashion Farm Boutique offers stylish and comfortable clothing and accessories for women from sizes S-3XL.  The store offers unique pieces and gift options.
