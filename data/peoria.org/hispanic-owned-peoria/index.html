Discover Hispanic-Owned Peoria

It is no secret that the Hispanic culture and business community are living and thriving in the Greater Peoria area. Peoria has been welcoming and supportive of new businesses, and Hispanic businesses are participants in the economic growth the city has experienced in recent years. The demand for Hispanic businesses has been growing and shows no signs of slowing down. As the Hispanic community increases, so do the needs they have as consumers throughout the city and, in turn, the sharing of culture through restaurants, retail and the hospitality industry with residents and travelers alike. Discover Peoria is happy to highlight Hispanic-owned businesses that have contributed to the growth of the region and continue to make a positive impact on the lives of not only the people that own them but also the people they serve. 

For more information or to recommend other businesses to include, email info@peoria.org.
