Santa Sightings in Greater Peoria

By Guest Writer: Annalee Anderson on Nov. 23, 2022

Santa Claus is comin’ to town!! We’ve made a list of scheduled Santa stops in the Greater Peoria Area before he heads back to the North Pole. Check out these locations and events for photo ops with Saint Nick.

 

Note:  Many of the free and candid Santa photo ops are usually BYOC (Bring Your Own Camera). We’ll keep updating this list for Santa photo opportunities as submitted, but for the full listing of holiday experiences in the Greater Peoria Area, check out our Calendar of Events.   



 

PHOTOS WITH SANTA

Bass Pro Shops  {East Peoria}   //   November 5 - December 24, 2022, Mon-Fri @ 5pm - 8pm, Sat @ 10am-8pm, Sun @ 10am - 5pm

 

Hoerr Nursery  {Peoria}   //   November 26 + December 3, 2022  @ 11am - 2pm   //   Pets Welcomed

 

Northwoods Mall  {Peoria}   //   November 25 - December 24, 2022, Mon-Sat  @ 11am - 7pm, Sun @ 9am-5pm

 

PetSmart   {Peoria + East Peoria}   //   December 4-5, December 11-12, + December 18-19, 2022  @ 12pm-3pm   //   Reservations Recommended, Pets Welcomed

 

Snowman's Reindeer Farm   {Canton}   //   November 1 - December 26, 2022   //   Reservations Only

 

SOS Home   {Washington}   //   December 17, 2022 @ 10am-12pm

 

The Shoppes at Grand Prairie  {Peoria}   //   November 25 - December 24, 2022,  Every Weekend @ 12pm-5pm

135th Santa Claus Parade  {Peoria}   //   November 25, 2022

Breakfast with Santa   {Lacon, Club LaCon}   //   December 3, 2022  @ 7:30-10:30am

 

Donuts with Santa   {Manito, The Forman Center}   //   December 3, 2022  @ 9:00-11:00am

 

El Paso Christmas Walk  {El Paso, Jefferson Park Gym}   //   December 3, 2022  @ 8:00-1:00pm

 

Christmas in the Village   {Peoria, Peoria Riverfront Museum}   //   December 3, 2022  @ 2-8pm

 

Christmas Walk   {Elmwood}   //   December 3, 2022  @ 4-7pm

 

Santa Breakfast   {Washington}   //   December 3, 2022  @ 9-11am

 

Breakfast with Santa + Mrs. Claus  {Pekin, Avanti’s Event Center}   //   December 10 + 17, 2022 @ 7:30-8:30am, 9-10am, or 10:30-11:30am   //   Reservations Only

 

St. Thomas Advent Market  {Peoria Heights}   //   December 3, 2022 @ 11am-1pm

 

Breakfast + Photos with Santa  {East Peoria, Quail Meadows Golf Course}   //   December 3, 2022 @ 8am-11am   //   Reserve time slot by November 30, 2022

 

Santa’s Woodland Workshop  {Chillicothe, Camp Wokanda}   //   December 3-4, 2022

 

Breakfast with Santa   {Peoria, Hy-Vee Orange Prairie Rd}   //   December 4 and December 18, 2022 @ 8am-12pm

 

A Village Christmas   {Metamora}   //   December 4, 2022 @ 12pm-3pm

 

Northwoods Mall Pet Photos  {Peoria}   //   December 5, 2022  @ 4-7pm

Santa in the Greenhouse  {Dunlap, Green View Companies}   //   December 10, 2022  @ 11am - 2pm
